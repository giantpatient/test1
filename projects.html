Our project explained

ORIGINE OF THE PROJECT

Many scholarships are offered every year in Community Colleges and Universities in Florida. Some of Togolese Students are among the recipients of these scholarships. We are convinced that the creation of a Scholarship funded by Togolese (individual and business) will be a great source of inspiration, support and reward for Togolese students who are dedicated to pursue excellence in their Education. â€‹The only way to see this happen is to take action and to begin to raise fund to establish "Togolese Students Scholarships.

NAME OF THE SCHOLARSHIP

Togolese Students Scholarships

COLLEGES AND UNIVERSITIES COVERED

Togolese Students Scholarships will be established in Community Colleges and Universities in Florida. It will be based on the number of Togolese Students in theses Colleges and Universities. Togolese Students can request a scholarship to be fund in their Colleges or Universities. We will make decision based on the resources available at the time of the request.

HILLSBOROUGH COMMUNITY COLLEGE : BEGINNING POINT

Even though our vision is to cover community Colleges and University in Florida, we cannot establish Scholarship in all of them now. We choose Hillsborough Community College as beginning point for this ambitious project.

UNIVERSITY OF SOUTH FLORIDA : OUR NEXT STEP.

After Hillsborough Community College, we will establish a scholarship at USF -Tampa.

THE OTHER COLLEGES AND UNIVERSITIES

We will cover these colleges and Universities as we grow.

FREQUENCY OF THE SCHOLARSHIP

FALL & SPRING OF EVERY ACADEMIC YEAR
